How to play Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

The 5 gestures

  • 🪨 Rock — closed fist
  • 📄 Paper — open hand
  • ✂️ Scissors — two fingers
  • 🦎 Lizard — hand like a puppet
  • 🖖 Spock — Vulcan salute

The 10 win relationships

  • 🪨 Rock crushes ✂️ and 🦎
  • 📄 Paper covers 🪨 and disproves 🖖

Objective

Popularized by The Big Bang Theory, RPSLS adds Lizard and Spock to the classic game. With 10 win relationships instead of 3, draws are rarer and strategy runs deeper.
